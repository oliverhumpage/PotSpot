PotSpot
Snooker ball detector for BBC iPlayer
Version 1.1

About

PotSpot detects snooker balls in BBC iPlayer livestreams and renders an interactive top-down plan view of the table. Click any ball to see aim lines and object-ball deflections.

Detection runs entirely in the browser — no data leaves your device apart from completely anonymous usage stats (basically, how many times Detect gets clicked).
